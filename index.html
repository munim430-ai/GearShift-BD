OUR STORY

ABOUT GEARSHIFT BD

GearShift BD was founded by passionate riders, for riders. We understand the road — the thrill, the risks, and the need for gear you can trust. From helmets that protect to accessories that perform, every product in our collection is handpicked with riders in mind.

Based in Bangladesh, we serve riders from Dhaka to Chittagong, Sylhet to Rajshahi. Our mission is simple: make premium motorcycle accessories accessible to every Bangladeshi rider at honest prices.

Whether you're a daily commuter, weekend tourer, or track enthusiast — GearShift BD has everything you need to Gear Up. Shift Up. Ride On.
